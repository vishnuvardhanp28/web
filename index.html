About ROCS

ACM India ROCS (Research Opportunities in Computer Science) is designed to inspire undergraduate students to explore research careers in both academia and industry. The event features talks by leading researchers from premier institutions, covering diverse areas of computer science including theoretical foundations, systems, artificial intelligence, and software engineering.


This event provides a unique opportunity for students to interact with experts, understand different research domains, and get guidance on pursuing higher education and research-oriented careers.
