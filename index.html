About the Initiative

ACM India ROCS (Research Opportunities in Computer Science) is a flagship initiative designed to introduce undergraduate students to the diverse and exciting world of research.

Hosted by SRM University AP, this event brings together leading experts from IITs and top research institutions to share their journey and insights. The goal is to demystify research careers and inspire students to pursue innovation in academia and industry.

  • Insightful talks by field experts
  • Direct interaction with researchers
  • Guidance on PhD and research careers
  • Understanding the research landscape in India
