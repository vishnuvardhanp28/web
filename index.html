What is ROCS?

ACM India ROCS (Research Opportunities in Computer Science) is an initiative aimed at introducing undergraduate students to the exciting world of research in Computer Science.

Through a series of talks by leading experts, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities, ROCS aims to inspire students to pursue research careers in academia and industry.

  • Expert Talks & Keynotes
  • Networking with Peers
  • Research Career Guidance
  • Interactive Q&A Sessions
